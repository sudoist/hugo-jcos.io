Virtual Mae

One of the projects with Dart. This involves lots of customization to themes and especially WooCommerce.

The projects is an online shop but for registered members only. Since this is a niche site and shop a customized wish-list was placed to inquire about items. Built using Bootstrap, Parallax, Masonry Galleries and other design requests this is one of the bandwidth-heavy sites I’ve created. Forgot to mention that videos was used as background for home and contact pages.

End product was a working E-Commerce site that accepts and processes orders by direct communication of the owner and clients.

Site was built with WordPress for Content Management, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap.

Skills: Java, Javascript, JQuery, AJAX, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL, Centos
